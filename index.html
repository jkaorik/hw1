A passion for cooking

Hi, welcome to my blog. My name is Kim and I love experimenting in new flavors and recipes in my kitchen. I grew up in a small farm, in the middle of the mountains, surrounded by rivers and trees. As I played in the yard, a delicious smell came out through the window. A smell of spices, powerful, tender... home cooking. As I watched my grandmother cook in the wood stove, a passion emerged.

Inspirations

Growing up among two different cultures, the Latin American and the Asian, my cuisine could not be less diverse. Here in this blog I try to bring not just part of my "memorie cuisine", but also share new references from places and people I meet in my worldwide travels.You should expect a touch of Mediterranea, Asian Fusion, and especially Brazilian cuisine. Mostly simple cooking, but with a touch of the classics.

Partners and Collaborators

Missy Kamaro, Antonia Silva, Kinue Yakashima, Fazenda Gennaro,Kai Fishmarket... some of the inumerous partners and collaborators you will find here. I believe food is about connections, about exchange, and good ingredients. That is why I partner with the best local food suppliers, and count with the collaboration of talented and differentiated home chefs.
